# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

0XB20 Laboratory welcomes practical, respectful technical collaboration.

Participants are expected to:

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Critique code and reasoning, not people.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Respect security-sensitive disclosure.
- Avoid harassment, threats, spam, or targeted abuse.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Publishing another person's private information.
- Sharing private keys, seed phrases, or credentials.
- Harassment, hate speech, or sustained disruption.
- Social engineering attempts against maintainers or contributors.
- Submitting malicious code or intentionally unsafe wallet behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or reject contributions that violate this code.

Security-sensitive reports should follow `SECURITY.md`.
